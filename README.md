Here are **20 features of a canteen billing system**:

1. **User-friendly interface** – Easy navigation for cashiers and staff.
2. **Item catalog management** – Add, update, or remove food items and categories.
3. **Price management** – Set prices, discounts, tax, and combos.
4. **Stock/Inventory tracking** – Automatically reduce stock with each sale.
5. **Fast billing & checkout** – Quick bill generation with minimal clicks.
6. **Barcode/QR code scanning** – Speed up item entry during checkout.
7. **Digital receipts** – Option to send receipts via SMS or email.
8. **Multiple payment options** – Cash, card, UPI, wallet, prepaid accounts.
9. **Customer account system** – Credit, prepaid balance, loyalty points.
10. **Daily sales reporting** – Summary of total sales, peak hours, etc.
11. **Cashier login & role management** – Different permissions for admin, staff, and manager.
12. **Order history & reprinting** – View past bills and reprint receipts.
13. **Tax & GST handling** – Automatically apply taxes.
14. **Offline mode support** – Continue billing without internet.
15. **Kitchen order printing (KOT)** – Send orders directly to kitchen printer or display.
16. **Meal plan/Subscription support** – For hostels, schools, or corporate canteens.
17. **Combo meal creation** – Combine several items at a discounted price.
18. **Refunds & void bill management** – Safe handling of cancelled or returned orders.
19. **Dashboard analytics** – Graphs showing sales trends and popular items.
20. **Data backup & security** – Regular backups and secure record keeping.
